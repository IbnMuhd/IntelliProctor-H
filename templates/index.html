About ExamGuard

ExamGuard is an advanced AI-powered proctoring platform designed to ensure exam integrity for online assessments.
It features real-time face verification, behavior monitoring, audio and screen activity detection, and instant alerts for suspicious actions.
The system provides dedicated dashboards for both students and administrators, making online exams secure and user-friendly.

  • Face registration and verification using AI
  • Real-time proctoring (multiple faces, head movement, audio, screen activity)
  • Admin and student dashboards
  • Automated grading and persistent alert history
