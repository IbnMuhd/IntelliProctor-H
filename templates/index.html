About ExamGuard

ExamGuard is an advanced AI-powered proctoring platform designed to ensure exam integrity for online assessments. Our system provides comprehensive monitoring solutions that give educators confidence in the validity of their online exams.

With real-time face verification, behavior monitoring, and activity detection, ExamGuard helps maintain academic integrity while providing a seamless experience for both students and administrators.

Key Features:

  • AI-Powered Face Recognition: Secure identity verification using facial recognition technology
  • Real-Time Monitoring: Detection of multiple faces, suspicious head movements, and unusual audio
  • Screen Activity Tracking: Monitoring for unauthorized applications or browser tabs
  • Comprehensive Dashboards: Intuitive interfaces for both students and administrators
  • Automated Reporting: Detailed logs of all proctoring events and alerts
